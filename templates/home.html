Welcome, Dweller! ^_^


This web-app was created to help out "Fallout 76" players manage their weapon inventory, as well as finding new weapons for their own collection.



Here you can perform following actions:


Search

Gives you an option to search through other users inventories. You can make a specific request, by choosing all or some selection options.

Add

This is the way to populate your own datatable with weapons you currently own. Application logic will prevent you from choosing and adding unobtainable prefixes for weapons.

Inventory

Here, you can view all the weapons you've added into your table. Within this table, you can remove weapons you don't need, by clicking "trash bin" icon on corresponding row.
